1. What is a key takeaway from the Datasaurus Dozen dataset when it comes to data visualization?
a. Visualizing data can reveal hidden patterns not apparent in summary statistics.
b. Summary statistics are always sufficient to understand data patterns.
c. Data visualization is unnecessary when summary statistics are available.
d. Summary statistics and data visualization yield identical results.

Correct!  a
2. What is one of the primary benefits of using data visualization in data analysis?

a. It helps in uncovering patterns and trends in the data.
b. It enhances the aesthetics of reports and presentations.
c. It provides a substitute for collecting and analyzing data.
d. Data visualization makes analysis more complex and time-consuming.

Correct! a
